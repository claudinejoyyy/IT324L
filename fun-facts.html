Here are some fun facts about animals that you probobly didn't know!

Cows can sleep standing up, but they can only dream lying down.

It is much easier for dogs to learn spoken commands if they are given in conjunction with hand signals or gestures.

Just one cow gives off enought harmful methane gas in a single day to fill around 400 litre bottles.

Did you know that cows have four stomaches?

The blue whale weights as much as thirty(30) elephants and is as long as greyhound buses.

Most elephats weigh less than the tongue of a blue whale.

Pandas don't have particular sleeping spots; they simply fall asleep whenever they happen to be.

A tiger's legs are so powerful that they can remain standing even when dead.

A male penguin offers a pebble to a female penguin. If she takes it, they become partners. Some penguins don't build nests, instead they carry their eggs on thier feet.

Pigs heart have been used in human heart transplants.
